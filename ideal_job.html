My Ideal Job


I have presented below an example of an ideal job for me. The position is for a Senior Software developer for JB Hi-Fi.

Link to job advertisement on the JB Hi-Fi Careers website.

Description of the position

I would be working as part of a team to develop software and other solutions for the retail chain JB HI-FI in this position. The main task of this job would be using programming to fix issues or create new solutions for all parties involved with the company; this would include improving existing applications and developing new ones for staff or customers to use. I would be involved with applications at different stages of their life cycles, pre and post-release, to ensure. This job appeals to me as it would make direct use of my passion for solving complex problems. Having worked with JB previously, I have enjoyed the company culture and have witnessed the team spirit that all departments can show when working. The different IT teams appeared to be cohesive and having a solid bond to achieve their common goals.

Qualifications and Skills required

While the ad does not mention it, I believe that a minimum of an IT Degree is needed for this position as it would offer you exposition to the base of the technical requirements. The primary skill required would be to know various programming languages but, more importantly, C# and Node.js, as mentioned in the ad. There are also requirements to understand multiple technologies such as cloud computing with exposition to Amazon Web Services and Microsoft Azure, which are currently the US's two most extensive cloud computing services (Canalys 2021). The ad also focuses on an event-driven architecture for their applications, so it will be advantageous to have experience with systems where monitoring events as triggers happens. It will be essential to know the different software development strategies such as Test-driven Development (TDD) and, subsequently, Behaviour-driven Development (BDD). Another critical part of the requirements is familiarity with relational databases for storing information and document databases. For soft skills, having good problem-solving abilities and being a solid team player would be essential to be successful in this position.

My Current Qualifications and Skills

As I haven't previously worked in the IT department, I, unfortunately, do not have the required technical skills to apply for this role. However, during my career, I have developed all the necessary character traits required. Being naturally introverted, I have worked hard to communicate openly in professional settings when needed. I have worked in different types of teams and understand that flexibility and adaptability are at the core of ensuring success for the team. My experience in the logistics industry has allowed me to demonstrate my problem-solving skills daily.

How I will obtain the required qualifications

As I already possess the primary soft skills and teamwork experience required, the main requirement I will be working on will be the technical qualifications over the next few years. I first intend to complete my Bachelor of IT with a particular focus on mastering various programming languages. I will also select subjects that will offer me exposure to the rest of the technical requirements, such as databases, software development, and cloud computing. As this is a Senior position, I may have to find employment in Junior positions first to gain the necessary work experience as a software developer.
